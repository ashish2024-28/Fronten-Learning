Narendra Modi

Born: September 17, 1950

Place of Birth:Vadnagar, Gujarat, India

Position:Prime Minister of India

About

Narendra Modi is the 14th and current Prime Minister of India, serving since May 2014. He is a member of the BJP and has previously served as the Chief Minister of Gujarat from 2001 to 2014. Known for his dynamic leadership, Modi has introduced several key initiatives including Digital India, Swachh Bharat, and Make in India.

Key Initiatives

  • Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Clean India Mission)
  • Digital India
  • Make in India
  • Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana
  • Ayushman Bharat
